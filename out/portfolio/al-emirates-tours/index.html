The Challenge

Al Emirates Tours is a UAE tour operator competing in a crowded Dubai tourism market. They needed a professional website showcasing their desert, city, and adventure tours while capturing direct bookings before visitors go to comparison aggregators.

What I Built

In the UAE tourism market, WhatsApp is as important as a booking form — customers expect to message instantly. I built Al Emirates Tours with WhatsApp CTAs on every tour page alongside traditional forms, giving the operator two conversion channels and the visitor their preferred method.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with UAE-inspired warm premium design
  • Tour listing pages: desert safari, city tours, adventure packages, and transfers
  • Individual tour pages with itinerary, pricing, inclusions, and exclusions
  • WhatsApp integration for direct enquiries — critical for the UAE market
  • Enquiry and booking forms optimised for the UAE mobile-dominant audience
  • SEO structure targeting Dubai tourism search keywords
  • Mobile-first build throughout
