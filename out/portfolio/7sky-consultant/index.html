The Challenge

7 Sky Consultancy is a study abroad specialist in Lahore with a 98% visa success rate and 94% scholarship placement record. They needed a website that converted enquiring students — a highly competitive market where trust and proven results are everything — into consultations and application clients.

What I Built

Study abroad consultancy is built on trust — students are making a life-changing decision and need to be convinced the agency has the track record to deliver. For 7 Sky, those numbers — 98% visa success, 94% scholarships — are genuinely remarkable. I built the site to lead with those results and then walk the student through exactly what the process looks like so the next step feels obvious.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with a professional, aspirational study abroad aesthetic
  • Country and university destination pages for the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and Europe
  • Results-forward hero section highlighting the 98% visa success and 94% scholarship rates
  • Step-by-step application process page to reduce the perceived complexity of studying abroad
  • Student testimonials and success stories from past clients
  • Service pages for visa consultation, application support, scholarship assistance, and pre-departure guidance
  • WhatsApp and contact form integration for immediate enquiry handling
