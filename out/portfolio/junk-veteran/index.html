The Challenge

Junk Veteran is a veteran-owned junk removal company in Massachusetts. They needed a local-SEO-focused site that converts quickly — junk removal is an urgent same-day need. The site had to rank for local search terms and get the phone ringing with minimal friction.

What I Built

Junk removal is hyper-local and often urgent. Someone needing their garage cleared is not going to read five paragraphs — they need a phone number in three seconds or they call a competitor. I built Junk Veteran with the phone number and service area as the first things a visitor sees.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with local SEO architecture from the start
  • Service pages for each junk type: furniture, appliances, construction debris, estate cleanouts
  • Location pages targeting Massachusetts cities and towns for local SEO
  • Prominent phone number and same-day service CTA above the fold on every page
  • Veteran-owned trust messaging throughout the site
  • Before and after project gallery to demonstrate service quality
  • Google Business Profile integration and review display section
