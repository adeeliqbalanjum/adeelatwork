The Challenge

Griffin Resources provides HR outsourcing, payroll, recruiting, and marketing services to small businesses. Their site needed to clearly communicate a wide service offering while making time-poor small business owners quickly understand the value and convert to a consultation.

What I Built

HR services sites are inherently complex — payroll, compliance, recruiting, and coaching each deserve their own explanation. I built Griffin Resources so a small business owner can identify their pain point in under 10 seconds and reach a consultation CTA without reading walls of text.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with a professional, corporate-but-approachable design
  • Service architecture covering payroll, recruiting, coaching, and marketing in clear sections
  • Lead-generation focused layout with consultation CTAs on every page
  • Trust section with testimonials and business metrics
  • Mobile-responsive design targeting busy business owners on the go
  • SEO-friendly structure for HR outsourcing and small business search terms
  • CRM form integration for lead capture and follow-up
