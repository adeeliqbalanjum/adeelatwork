The Challenge

Hercules Roof System is a roofing contractor serving Frisco and the wider DFW area. In a highly competitive local services market, their website needed to be found by local searchers, immediately convey trustworthiness, and convert visitors to quote requests — all on a mobile screen since most local service searches happen on phones.

What I Built

Roofing sites in Texas compete on credibility — after a hailstorm every contractor is promising the best job. Hercules Roof System needed a site that looked more established and trustworthy than the competition, with local SEO targeting the specific DFW zip codes they serve. I built it so a homeowner searching after a storm can find it, trust it, and request a quote in under two minutes.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with a strong, professional roofing industry aesthetic
  • Residential and commercial roofing service pages with clear scope and process explanations
  • Local SEO structure targeting Frisco, Plano, McKinney, and surrounding DFW suburbs
  • Prominent free quote CTA with phone number above the fold on every page
  • Insurance claim assistance page — a major conversion driver for storm damage roofing
  • Project gallery and before-after section to demonstrate craftsmanship
  • Google reviews integration and trust badge section
