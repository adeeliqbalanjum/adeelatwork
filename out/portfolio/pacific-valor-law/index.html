The Challenge

Pacific Valor Law is a VA-accredited disability attorney firm based in Okinawa serving overseas veterans with disability claims, appeals, and DIC cases. Their website needed to speak directly to veterans living abroad — a niche audience that often feels overlooked by US-based legal services — and make the free case review process feel accessible and trustworthy.

What I Built

Veterans living overseas face a double barrier when filing VA disability claims — distance from the US system and a lack of attorneys who understand their situation. Pacific Valor Law fills that gap. I built their site to speak directly to that audience: credible, clear about the process, and making the first step — a free case review — feel simple to take.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro build with a professional legal aesthetic that still feels approachable to veterans
  • Practice area pages for VA disability claims, appeals, DIC, and ratings increases
  • Prominent free case review CTA on every page — critical conversion point for legal sites
  • VA accreditation and credentials displayed prominently to establish legitimacy
  • Overseas veteran-specific messaging addressing the unique challenges of filing from abroad
  • FAQ section covering the claims process, timelines, and what to expect
  • Mobile-responsive build with fast load times for international audiences on varying connections
