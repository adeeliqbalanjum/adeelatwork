The Challenge

Griffin IT's unique selling proposition — stock your shelf for free, pay after you sell — was buried in a cluttered old website. They needed a clean, direct site putting this zero-risk model front and centre for IT resellers and MSPs.

What I Built

The pay-after-you-sell model is genuinely disruptive in IT hardware supply — MSPs can stock hardware without upfront capital risk. That message was lost on the old site. I rebuilt Griffin IT with that headline front and centre, supporting pages explaining the process in simple terms for a busy IT reseller.

  • Complete WordPress and Elementor rebuild with the unique stock model as the hero message
  • Clear above-the-fold value proposition: stock, sell, then pay
  • Service and product category pages for MSP hardware solutions
  • Designed for a B2B audience: IT resellers and managed service providers
  • Lead capture forms for partnership and wholesale enquiries
  • Performance optimisation across the WordPress installation
  • Plugin audit and conflict resolution on legacy installation
