The Challenge

Happy Hearts Children's Center is a Herndon-based daycare competing against national chains and local operators. Parents researching childcare are making one of the most important decisions of their lives — the site needed to be warm, trustworthy, and immediately answer the questions every parent has about safety, curriculum, and enrollment.

What I Built

Parents choosing childcare are reading between the lines of every website element — is this place safe, do they care, is the staff qualified. Happy Hearts answers all of those questions before the parent consciously asks them, through warm imagery, clear staff and licensing information, and an easy tour CTA.

  • WordPress and WP Rocket build with performance-critical configuration for image-heavy pages
  • Warm, friendly design that matches the brand — approachable without being childish
  • Program pages: infant care, toddler, preschool — each with age-appropriate curriculum detail
  • Tour scheduling CTA prominent on every page since enrollment starts with a visit
  • Parent-facing FAQ section covering safety, licensing, staff qualifications, and meals
  • Photo gallery of the facility and classrooms to build pre-visit confidence
  • WP Rocket configuration for sub-2-second loads on image-heavy pages
