The Challenge

Todd Malloy is a speaker, podcast host, and philosopher behind a personal growth framework called Toddism. His brand needed a website that communicated the depth of his philosophy while making it easy for podcast listeners, event organisers, and followers to engage — from reading his philosophy to booking him as a guest.

What I Built

Personal brand sites for speakers and thinkers need to do something most business sites do not — convey personality and depth while still being functional. For Todd, the site needed to make you feel his philosophy before you have read a single word, then give you clear paths whether you are a fan, a podcast producer, or an event organiser.

  • WordPress and Elementor Pro personal brand build centred on Todd's philosophy and content
  • Toddism philosophy page explaining the core principles in an engaging, visual layout
  • Podcast showcase section with episode listings and guest appearance archive
  • Speaker and podcast guest booking section for event organisers
  • Blog and content hub for ongoing philosophy and growth content
  • Social proof section with media appearances and featured podcast slots
  • Email opt-in integration for audience list building
